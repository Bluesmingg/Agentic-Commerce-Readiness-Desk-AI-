## Executive Summary

The storefront is readable for human buyers, but AI shopping agents may struggle with variant-level price accuracy, collection context, shipping-policy summarization, and mobile checkout affordances.

Five pages were reviewed: two product pages, one collection page, one policy page, and the cart. Two pages had Product JSON-LD, but both had warnings. Checkout handoff was functional in the sample data, but the mobile discount field was below the fold.

Highest priority work:

1. Fix stale Product/Offer structured data on variant change.
2. Add ItemList and BreadcrumbList schema to collection pages.
3. Convert shipping and returns policy prose into concise FAQ blocks.
4. Improve product facts for high-margin SKUs.
5. Move and label the mobile discount field in cart.
