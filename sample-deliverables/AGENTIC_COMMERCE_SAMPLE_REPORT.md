## Executive Summary

The storefront is readable for human buyers, but AI shopping agents may struggle with variant-level price accuracy, collection context, shipping-policy summarization and mobile checkout affordances.

5 pages were crawled: 2 product pages, 1 collection, 1 policy, and the cart. 2 pages had JSON-LD (both Product types), but both had warnings. The checkout handoff was functional but the discount field on mobile was below the fold.

Highest priority work:

1. **Fix stale Product/Offer structured data on variant change** (critical) — JSON-LD Offer.price is static, doesn't update when variant switches from $189 to $199.
2. **Add ItemList and BreadcrumbList schema to collection pages** (high) — AI shopping systems can't programmatically compare catalog products.
3. **Convert shipping and returns policy prose into concise FAQ blocks** (high) — agents can't reliably extract delivery windows or free-shipping thresholds.
4. **Improve product facts for high-margin SKUs** (medium) — Canvas Weekender missing brand, dimensions, material in title and JSON-LD.
5. **Move and label the mobile discount field in cart** (medium) — discount input below fold on mobile, no ARIA label.
